How to use
Portfolio Tracker · Quick Guide
➕ Adding a position
Use the ADD POSITION form at the bottom. Pick a type, enter the symbol, an optional label, and the quantity you hold.
₿ Crypto — Binance symbols
Use the Binance ticker without the USDT suffix. Prices update in real-time via WebSocket.
BTC — Bitcoin
ETH — Ethereum
SOL — Solana
BNB — BNB
XRP — Ripple
DOGE — Dogecoin
📈 US Stocks — Yahoo Finance ticker
Just use the plain ticker. Prices update every 15 seconds via Yahoo Finance.
AAPL — Apple
NVDA — NVIDIA
MSFT — Microsoft
TSLA — Tesla
🇪🇺 European Stocks & ETFs — add exchange suffix
European stocks need a suffix to identify the exchange. Find the exact ticker on finance.yahoo.com and copy it exactly.
VWCE.DE — Xetra
EDP.LS — Lisbon
SAN.MC — Madrid
ENI.MI — Milan
Common suffixes: .DE Xetra · .LS Lisbon · .MC Madrid · .AS Amsterdam · .PA Paris · .MI Milan · .L London
🔍 Finding the right ticker
Go to finance.yahoo.com → search for your stock or ETF → use the ticker shown in the URL or next to the name.
Example: searching "Vanguard All World" shows VWCE.DE for the Xetra-listed version.
